# MoodsCopyTest: Creative Brief

## The Hook
A dual-channel spatial playground. Capture fleeting micro-loops and wash them in lush, evolving effects to create endless ambient textures and unexpected happy accidents.

## The Vision
MoodsCopyTest is inspired by the legendary Chase Bliss MOOD MkII. It features two interconnected channels:
1. **The Wet Channel:** Offers spatial and temporal effects including Reverb, Delay, and Slip (pitch shifting/time manipulation).
2. **The Micro-Looper:** An "always-listening" channel that captures audio snippets and manipulating them through stretching, tape degradation, or envelope triggering.

These two channels interact dynamically. The defining feature is the **Clock** control, which globally adjusts the sample rate, acting as a master control for loop length, fidelity, playback speed, and pitch. This allows you to fluidly shift from pristine, high-resolution ambiance to gritty, lo-fi soundscapes.

## Vibe & Aesthetic
A sophisticated, modern boutique pedal interface. The layout is divided into two distinct halves representing the Wet and Looper channels. Soft pastel tones with dark, elegant metallic knobs, emphasizing a tactile, interactive "playable" feel.
