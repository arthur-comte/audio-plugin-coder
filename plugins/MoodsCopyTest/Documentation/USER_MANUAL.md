# MoodsCopyTest User Manual

Welcome to **MoodsCopyTest**, a boutique audio plugin inspired by modern classic pedals.

## Overview
MoodsCopyTest is a two-channel spatial and micro-looping effect. It features a lush **Wet Channel** for reverb and delay, and a high-fidelity **Micro-Looper** for capturing and transforming audio snippets.

## The Wet Channel (Deep Pink)
- **TIME**: Controls the size of the reverb or the length of the delay/slip buffer.
- **MODIFY**: 
  - **Reverb**: Controls damping and diffusion.
  - **Delay**: Controls feedback and filter color.
  - **Slip**: Controls the pitch shift amount.

## The Micro-Looper (Mint Green)
- **LENGTH**: Sets the duration of the loop snippet.
- **MODIFY**:
  - **Tape**: Controls playback speed and direction.
  - **Env**: Handles stutter and windowing behavior.
  - **Stretch**: Manages granular time-stretching density.

## Global Controls
- **CLOCK**: Scales the internal sample rate, affecting the quality and timing of both channels simultaneously.
- **MIX**: Blends the overall wet signal with the dry input.

## Routing
MoodsCopyTest supports two routing modes:
- **Serial**: The output of the Wet channel feeds into the Looper (or vice versa).
- **Parallel**: Both channels process the input independently and are mixed at the output.
