# NewMood - Creative Brief

## Hook
**Transform any sound into evolving ambient textures with the spirit of the legendary Chase Bliss Mood mkII — now as a VST3 plugin with DAW sync.**

## Description

NewMood is a granular looper/ambient effect that captures the essence of the Chase Bliss Mood mkII pedal. It creates lush, evolving soundscapes through time-stretched loops, granular processing, and organic modulation.

### Core Features

1. **Granular Looper**
   - Records and loops audio with variable length (Time control)
   - Applies granular processing with adjustable grain size (Speed control)
   - Mixes between dry and wet signals

2. **DAW Clock Sync**
   - Syncs loop length to DAW tempo
   - Supports musical subdivisions (1/4, 1/8, 1/16, 1/32, triplets)
   - Manual tap tempo option

3. **Chase Bliss Aesthetic**
   - Hand-drawn, organic visual design
   - Warm, tactile feel reminiscent of hardware
   - Unique character that stands out from clinical plugins

### Target Users
- Electronic music producers seeking ambient textures
- Sound designers creating evolving pads and atmospheres
- Guitarists wanting to explore ambient territories
- Any musician looking to add organic movement to their sound

### Use Cases
- Creating ambient backgrounds from simple chords
- Transforming rhythmic parts into atmospheric washes
- Generating unique textures for film/game audio
- Live performance with DAW sync
